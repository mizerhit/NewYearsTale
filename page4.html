Challenge Two: A Test of Kindness

For the second challenge, Lumos presented Ada with a simulation of a bustling town square filled with virtual characters. Some were sad, others angry, and a few were simply bored. Ada’s task was to tweak Spark’s code to brighten their moods.

She added features like cheerful jokes, thoughtful compliments, and even a button to share uplifting stories. As she watched the virtual characters smile and laugh, Ada felt her heart glow.

“You have the gift of empathy,” said Lumos. “Spark will shine brighter because of it.”
